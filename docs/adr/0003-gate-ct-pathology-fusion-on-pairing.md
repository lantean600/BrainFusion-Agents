# Gate CT-pathology fusion on verified pairing

Status: accepted

CT-pathology fusion will only become a formal experiment after patient-level or lesion-level pairing is verified for the selected cancer cohort. Until then, CT and WSI are separate branches, and CT-pathology work is limited to feasibility review, pairing audit, and extension design.

## Consequences

- Dataset-level co-availability is not enough to claim multimodal pairing.
- CT-pathology is described as cross-scale fusion, not pixel-level fusion.
- Missing-aware CT/WSI fusion is allowed only after the pairing gate passes.
